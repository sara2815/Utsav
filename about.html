UTSAV

The Home away from Home

Created in 1980, UTSAV - UBC Indian Students Association is proud to be the largest student-run club at UBC and we strive to nurture a welcoming atmosphere for every student here. We resonate with embracing the true spirit of South Asian culture and aim to help students identify with their roots by instilling a sense of home away from home.

Each semester we organize events representing South Asian culture such as Diwali and Holi, with a footfall ranging from 500 to 4000 people. We are open to all students, regardless of race or religion-
All you need is an interest in all things Indian!
